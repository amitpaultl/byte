CyberTube

CyberTube initially started its journey with the name of Orika Corporation with effect from 25 th February, 2013. Later on, it was re-named as “CyberTube” from 1st January 2022. CyberTube is a trading company that imports and exports building materials, such as crushed stone, limestone, and rock. The company's vision is to be a leading supplier of building materials in Bangladesh, and to contribute to the country's infrastructure development.
